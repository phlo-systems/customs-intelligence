Features

Everything your trade desk needs

One platform for duty calculation, classification, compliance, and cost optimisation.

📈

Landed Cost Calculator

Instant duty, tax, and total landed cost for any HS code, origin, and destination. Includes FTA preferences, anti-dumping duties, and excise.

🤖

AI Classification

Describe your product in plain English, upload a spec sheet, or snap a photo. Our AI returns the correct HS code with confidence scoring.

💰

Duty Savings Finder

Automatically scans your trade routes for FTA savings, duty reductions, and drawback refunds. Personalised to your ERP data.

📣

Real-time Alerts

Tariff changes, expiring preferences, new anti-dumping duties, sanctions updates. Know before it hits your shipment.

📄

Document Checklist

Country-specific import documents, certificates, and permits. Never miss a phytosanitary cert or ANVISA registration again.

🔗

ERP Integration

Connect Xero, Acumatica, or any ERP. Auto-classify your products, track supplier concentration, and detect spending trends.
